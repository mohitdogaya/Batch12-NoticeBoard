About Batch 12

Batch 12 is a group of passionate learners enrolled in the ITEP (Information Technology Excellence Program) supported by InfoBeans Foundation. Our goal is to build strong technical and soft skills to thrive in the IT industry.

Guided by experienced mentors and powered by dedication, Batch 12 is known for its unity, collaboration, and ambition. We focus on web development, programming, communication, and real-world problem solving to become industry-ready professionals.
